# Tristar Locksmith — Product Context

## register: brand

## Product

Tristar Locksmith is a local mobile locksmith serving Knoxville, TN and 27 East
Tennessee cities. The website's primary goal is to convert paid-search clicks into
phone calls and form-submitted leads. Revenue is phone calls. Emergencies drive
the highest intent and willingness-to-pay.

## Audience

Someone locked out of their car, house, or business — stressed, mobile, often
outside. They searched Google from their phone 60 seconds ago. They want ONE thing:
a real local person who will come to them now and tell them the price before they
start. They do not want to read. They do not want to scroll. They want to tap Call.

## Tone & Voice

- Direct, calm, confident — no shouting
- Local and human ("Our team", "our technicians") — never corporate
- No 24/7 on routine service — "emergency service available around the clock"
- No superlatives ("best", "#1") without a source
- Pricing transparency: "Upfront Pricing", "price before we start"
- Trust through specifics: insured, background-checked, non-destructive entry

## Conversion hierarchy (phone > form > scroll)

1. One dominant tap-to-call CTA above the fold — red, pulsing, full-width on mobile
2. Short lead form (name + phone first, progressively expand)
3. Trust proof above the fold (5.0★ · 127 reviews inline)
4. City personalization: IP-geo → show visitor's city in H1 if it's a service area

## Competitive insight

- **HNM Locksmith:** city-in-headline via Google Ads Location Insertion + fast
  load, minimal friction. Appears prominently because of message relevance match.
- **locksmith-dispatch.com:** pure phone-first, no form, zero friction — high call
  intent, but no lead capture.
- **Our edge:** 5.0★/127 reviews (better proof than competitors), local (not
  national dispatch), upfront pricing, no-damage entry guarantee.

## What does NOT convert

- Forms with more than 2 required fields above the fold
- Buried phone numbers
- Generic headlines without the city name
- Low Quality Score from ad→LP copy mismatch (LP H1 must echo campaign keywords)
