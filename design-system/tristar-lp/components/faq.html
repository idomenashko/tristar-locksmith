Common Questions

Straight answers before we come out — that's how we operate.

How much does car key replacement cost?

It depends on the year, make, and model of your vehicle. We give you a firm price on the phone before we come out — no surprises.

Will you damage my car or door to get in?

No. We use professional non-destructive tools. We do not break windows or drill locks unless there is absolutely no other way, and we tell you before we do anything.

Do you serve my city?

We serve Knoxville and 27 surrounding East Tennessee cities, including Farragut, Maryville, Oak Ridge, Powell, Sevierville, and more. Call us and we'll confirm.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Tristar Locksmith is licensed and insured in the state of Tennessee.

What's the difference between rekeying and replacing a lock?

Rekeying changes the internal pins so old keys no longer work — it's less expensive. Replacing the lock is a new lock entirely. We can do both and recommend the right option for your situation.
