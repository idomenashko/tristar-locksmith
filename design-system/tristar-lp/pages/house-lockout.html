Common Questions

Straight answers. No runaround.

Will you damage my door to get it open?

No. We open doors non-destructively whenever possible. We do not drill unless there is no other option, and we tell you before we do anything.

How do I know I can trust you?

We ask for ID and proof of residency before we open any home. We're licensed and insured in Tennessee. You can also check our 127 verified reviews.

Can you rekey my lock while you're there?

Yes. If you want to rekey your lock on the same visit, we can usually do that. Just ask when you call.

What does a house lockout cost?

We give you a firm price on the phone before we come out. The exact rate depends on the lock type. No surprises.

What if I lost my only house key?

We can get you back inside and discuss your options — rekeying, new lock, or both. We'll walk you through what makes sense.
